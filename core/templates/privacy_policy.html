Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Rentalia, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our car rental service. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our service, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Driver's license information
  • Address and location data
  • Profile pictures (if provided)

Usage Information

  • Rental history and preferences
  • Device information and IP address
  • Browser type and settings
  • Usage patterns and interactions with our service

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage your vehicle rentals
  • Verify your identity and eligibility to rent vehicles
  • Process payments and maintain billing records

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our business
  • Law enforcement when required by law

  • Payment processors for secure transaction handling

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Request data portability
